Question:
what is the difference between the sego G and the G1. which Sebo is best for allergies?
Answer:

The SEBO Essential G series offers two models, the G1 and the G2. The major difference between the two is the width of the power head. The Essential G1 includes a 12-inch power head, while the Essential G2 features an extended, 15-inch power head. The Essential G series offers "Hospital Grade S Class Filtration". This means Sebo's bags have multiple layers of filtration paired with an S Class Micro filter which combine to equal a 99.9% effective rate in absorbing particles down to 0.3 microns.

About AllergyBuyersClub (from the business)

Description
Shop a curated selection of healthy home products including air purifiers, dehumidifiers, humidifiers, vacuums, and more. Enjoy free shipping over $75, customer reviews, and chat via online or phone with experts for free to help choose the right products.
History
Since 1998, Allergy Buyers Club has been reviewing and selling top-rated healthy home products to customers that suffer from allergies, asthma, and more. We are Better Business Bureau accredited and are an authorized online retailer of manufacturers in the air purification space.
